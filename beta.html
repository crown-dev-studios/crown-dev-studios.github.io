crown.dev is a software development studio in Brooklyn run by Marvin Charles and Justin Williams.

We specialize in working closely with clients to design and develop fit-for-purpose experiences for their users. We have first hand-experience building software for Trading, Consumer Finance, and Two-Sided Marketplaces.

We met as interns at Goldman Sachs where we both worked in the technology division. Since then we've honed our skills working on various projects such as Marquee Trader, Marcus, Louisa, Handy, and CashApp.
